\# BridgeCast  

Multi‑Generation Identity Substrate (Gen 1 → Gen 4)



BridgeCast is a structured repository containing the research papers, conceptual frameworks, and foundational artifacts that form the basis of a multi‑generation identity and continuity architecture.  

This repository serves as the canonical archive for the evolution of the BridgeCast system from Gen 1 (MSA‑anchored) through future generations.
